HOME DOMINANCE

During Boise State's recent streak of conference championships, Albertsons Stadium has proven to be a tough place for opponents. As of November 9, 2019, the Broncos are 126–9 (.933) at home since the 1999 season. The Broncos won 47 straight home conference games from 1999 to 2011 and were undefeated in conference during their 10 years in the WAC (40–0). The Broncos are 121–7 (.945) in regular season home games since 1999, and had a winning streak of 65 regular season games from 2001 to 2011.

LOCATION

Albertsons Stadium is located at the east end of the BSU campus, bordered by Broadway Avenue to the east, University Drive to the south, and the Boise River to the north. The elevation of its playing field is 2,695 feet (820 m) above sea level.
